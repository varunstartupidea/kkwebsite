About me

I bring over 16 years of cross-industry experience across Sales, Marketing, Project Sales, and Business Development in India and the Middle East — spanning Automobile, FMCG, Mattress, and Real Estate sectors. I have led institutional and channel sales, built distributor and dealer networks, and partnered with key stakeholders to drive multi-digit year-on-year growth in revenue, market share, and ROI.

I founded KK Talks with a clear mission: to help buyers and investors make safe, informed, and long-term real estate decisions based on ground reality, infrastructure growth, and risk awareness, rather than marketing hype. My advisory work focuses on plots and land investments, growth corridors, and long-term value creation over short-term speculation.

I follow a solution-based, advisory-first approach aimed at creating win-win outcomes and long-term client relationships, not one-time transactions. With strong expertise in strategic planning, sales analytics, channel partner alliances, and cross-cultural stakeholder management, I bring corporate discipline together with on-ground real estate insights.
